USA: All Cause Mortality During the Covid Pandemic Compared to Covid Mortality in the same time period (4 Jan 2020-3 July 2021)

Even the most at risk age groups are still 7 times more likely to die from other causes than from Covid.

  • A : Covid Mortality (COVID Deaths/Population of age group, in %)
  • B : All Cause Mortality (All Death/Population of age group, in %)
  • C : All Cause Mortality minus Covid Mortality (Non-Covid Deaths/Population of age group, in %)
  • D : Relative Risk (Covid/All Cause Mortality)
  • E : Equivalent Months of Death
  • F : How many times more likely someone is to die from something other than Covid-19.
